# Retail Sales & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed using the Superstore dataset to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, and product trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions through intuitive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Track revenue trends over time.
* Compare sales performance across regions.
* Provide actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Features:

* Total Revenue KPI
* Total Profit KPI
* Total Orders KPI
* Profit Margin KPI
* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Revenue by Category
* Revenue by Region
* Interactive Filters (Slicers)

### 2. Product Performance Analysis

Features:

* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Top 10 Products by Profit
* Sales Distribution by Sub-Category
* Profit Contribution by Category
* Product Performance KPIs

## Key Business Insights

* Identified the highest revenue-generating products.
* Analyzed profit contribution across product categories.
* Evaluated regional sales performance.
* Monitored overall business growth through revenue trends.
* Built an interactive dashboard to support business decision-making.
